⚽ Kyiv children's football club "Champion" 🏆

Age group 2013

Is your child fond of football and dreams of becoming a great player? Or maybe your son just likes team games, but hasn't yet decided what he would like to do in the future? Help your son to fulfill his dream and find himself, sign him up for a trial lesson in our club. We promise that he will fall in love with football and find great friends. "Champion" is not just a team, it's one big family!

Our motto: "Winning doesn't always mean being first. Victory is when you became better than you were".
